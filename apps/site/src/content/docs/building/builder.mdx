# The builder

The builder is where a workflow takes shape. A pillar rail — Trigger, Context,
Agent, Instructions — sits alongside focused editors for each, with the AI
copilot docked on the right. Edits autosave with a debounce, then trigger a
**dry-run compile** whose diagnostics are routed back onto the individual pillar
cards, so problems surface exactly where you'd fix them.

The instructions editor (CodeMirror 6) offers `@`-reference autocomplete into
trigger data and your context, and marks dangling references inline. When a draft
is ready, **Publish** compiles and builds it; **Run draft** publishes and opens
a fresh chat session against the new version in one step.
